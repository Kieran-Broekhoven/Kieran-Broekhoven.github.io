Every Item Counts

Funding is never guaranteed, and private donations may not be enough. Budget cuts have put shelters in great need of everyday supplies.

The Purple Project maintains an active wish-list filled with actively updated needs, sent to us directly by emergency shelters. By purchasing an item off the wish-list, you are immediately alleviating not only, financial burdens, but providing direct support and resources to those fleeing domestic abusers.

On a given day, domestic violence shelters in the state of California serve about 5,500 victims of domestic violence. However, in the same period, over 1,000 requests for help go unmet due to lack of funding and resources. Source: National Network to End Domestic Violence

Join us at the Purple Project as we work to bridge the gap between funding and safety by visiting our Amazon wish list page which lists items of current need at our local domestic violence shelters.
