We Are the Purple Project

By donating, you are able to help The Purple Project purchase items for domestic violence shelters and victims that are in immediate need.

A community that cares

The Purple Project hosted a donation drive at Bowls N Brews on February 15th. We gathered items from 11am-2pm. An estimated $1200 worth of items gathered at the drive were distributed to emergency domestic shelters in the greater San Diego area.
