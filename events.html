Events

Namaste at Home

March 3rd, 2021

To kick off 2021, The Purple Project is hosting a virtual Vinyasa Flow yoga class. Taught by certified yoga instructor Lynn Salas, admission is by donation with the goal of raising $5000 to help support our mission.



Light the Night

Dec 26th, 2020

On The Purple Project's one-year anniversary, hotels and business were asked to turn their up lights purple as a way to stand with domestic violence shelters and survivors of domestic abuse. We were pleased to have pledges from hotels downtown. We are thankful to have a community stand together to support those in need.



May the Fourth Be With You - Star Wars Trivia

May 4th 2020

The Purple Project celebrated May 4th (Star Wars Day) with a trivia competition. Proceeds from this event were able to provide two San Diego shelters with much-needed bedding and food supplies.

Comedy for a Cause

March 12th, 2020

The Purple Project, in partnership with Comedy Heights, ran a comedy show on March 12th to raise funds for shelters. Despite moving the event inside due to rain, we raised over $500 to help support our cause.

Drive for Hope

February 15th, 2020

The Purple Project hosted a donation drive at Bowls N Brews on February 15th. We gathered items from 11am-2pm. An estimated $1200 worth of items gathered at the drive were distributed to emergency domestic shelters in the greater San Diego area.
