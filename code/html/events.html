May the Fourth Be With You - Star Wars Trivia

May 4th 2020

Calling all fans of the Skywalker Saga! The Purple Project is celebrating May 4th (Star Wars Day) with a trivia competition. The questions will be streamed on YouTube live, and a form on HubSpot will be used to answer questions - we'll send you the details the day of! Trivia will include knowledge from all eleven movies so make sure you're caught up! Check out the Facebook event, and get your tickets on Eventbrite!


Comedy for a Cause

March 12th, 2020

The Purple Project, in partnership with Comedy Heights, ran a comedy show on March 12th to raise funds for shelters. Despite moving the event inside due to rain, we raised over $500 to help support our cause.

Drive for Hope

February 15th, 2020

The Purple Project hosted a donation drive at Bowls N Brews on February 15th. We gathered items from 11am-2pm. An estimated $1200 worth of items gathered at the drive were distributed to emergency domestic shelters in the greater San Diego area.
