Drive for Hope

February 15th, 2020

The Purple Project hosted a donation drive at Bowls N Brews on February 15th. We gathered items from 11am-2pm. An estimated $600 worth of items gathered at the drive were distributed to emergency domestic shelters in the greater San Diego area.
