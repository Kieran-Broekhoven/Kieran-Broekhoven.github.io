Drive for Hope

The Purple Project is hosting a donation drive at Bowls N Brews on February 15th. We will be gathering items from 11am-2pm. Items gathered at the drive will be distributed to emergency domestic violence shelters in the greater San Diego area.


Bring a friend, bring an item and most importantly bring your support! If you are not able to make it to the donation drive please visit our wishlist page. Items purchased off this wishlist are delivered to shelters in need.
